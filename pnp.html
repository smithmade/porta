What do those words painted on the front of PORTA mean?

Porta patens esto. Nulli claudatur honesto. Six Latin words, two complete sentences.This is the full name of PORTA, roughly translated as, "Be the door always open. Be it not closed to any honest person."

Then what's PORTA NATIONAL PARK?

The park (PNP as we like to call it) is Porta's outdoor seating/gaming area and a sub-brand of PORTA. It is most active during the late spring, summer and fall, when we're able to open up PORTA's garage doors and serve drinks outside. In the winter we use the park to sell Christmas trees felled in the forests of Oregon.

Cool. Can I bring my dog to the park?

We'd love to have dogs in the park, but it gets to be too much of a mess. So no dogs.

OK. Next question. How many seats does PORTA have?

Inside PORTA, we seat 180 people.

Is PORTA wheelchair-accessible?

Yes. Right through our front doors.

What's the dress code?

Casual, although our guests often like to fancy themselves up. In PORTA NATIONAL PARK during the summer, feel free to hang out in beach attire.

Where can I park?

You can park on the street in any available metered parking space.

Do you have gift certificates?

Yes we do. Just ask your hostess or server.

Can I bring in a cake?

We try to limit what is brought into PORTA so our guests can fully experience our food and craft in all its glory. However, for parties booked in our private room, we do allow cakes to find their way to our tables. Reach out to our Special Events Manager, Laura Cavallaro, with any questions you may have.

Do you add gratuity to the check?

A 20% gratuity is added to parties reserving our private room.

What credit cards do you accept?

American Express, Discover, MasterCard, and Visa.

Do you accept reservations?

Sorry, we only accept walk-ins. However, if you have a large party coming into PORTA, it's always good practice to call ahead and let our hostess know.

What is the usual wait time?

This varies from day to day and season to season, ranging anywhere from no wait to three hours. Feel free to call ahead and speak with our hostess to find out how busy we are.

Can you accommodate large parties?

PORTA is comprised of a main dining room with communal tables and a private room which can accommodate parties ranging from ten to 50 people. For all parties larger than 50 people, please contact our Special Events Manager, Laura Cavallaro, to support you with planning.

What time does the kitchen close?

This question requires a three-stage answer: Sunday through Wednesday, 11PM; Thursday, midnight; Friday and Saturday, 1AM.

What kind of food does Porta serve?

Contemporary, local, sustainable, Italian, American, everyman slow food. And that's our abridged answer.

Are there vegetarian and vegan options?

Yes. Most of our pastas and pizzas are vegetarian and we have several vegan options that appeal to even our most carnivorous of guests.

How about anything for celiacs?

Yes. In addition to our gluten-free salads and desserts that we serve everyday, we also offer gluten-free pizzas on Tuesdays. Our bar stocks several selections of gluten-free drinks as well, including Woodchuck Hard Cider and Estrella Daura.

Can I add or change any ingredients to a pizza or dish?

Sorry, you can only remove ingredients. We're very firm about this, so please don't try to make our servers feel like jerks for not giving in. Our recipes were created to bring about the best in our food. We stand by every ingredient for this reason.

Why can't I get a pizza to go?

We want to preserve the quality of our pizzas and your experience eating them, and this is best done by serving them fresh and hot from our ovens. Of course, you can always come in to PORTA to eat and take any leftovers you may have home with you.

Overview

Reserve one of PORTA’s unique spaces for a
rich, dramatic backdrop to any event: weddings
and ceremonies, photo shoots and filmings,
after parties, etc. A variety of spatial and seating
options can accommodate between 20 and 125
guests. Please contact our Events Coordinator for more information.

The Monk Room

Our smaller private space, The Monk Room is the perfect spot for hosting an intimate gathering that can always turn into a wild party.

Set Table Menu

PORTA’s signature menu for private
gatherings, the Set Table menu serves as a
foundation for choosing your event’s dining
options. Feel free to request anything you don’t
see listed on the menu and we'll do our best
to accommodate.

The Abbot Room

Our main party room, The Abbot Room can comfortably hold a crowd, whether they're dining, drinking, dancing, or hell-bent on
all three.
